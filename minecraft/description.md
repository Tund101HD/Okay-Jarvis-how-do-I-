This directory collects guides centered around **Minecraft development** — the toolchains, project templates, and IDE setups that turn "I have an idea for a mod" into "I have a `.jar` that doesn't crash on launch." It's split into two sub-topics:

- **[`mod-dev/`](./mod-dev)** — client/server mods (Forge, Fabric, NeoForge, etc.).
- **[`plugin-dev/`](./plugin-dev)** — server-side plugins (Bukkit, Spigot, Paper, and friends).

If you're here, you're likely trying to:

- Bootstrap a Minecraft project without spending an evening on `build.gradle` archaeology.
- Get your IDE to actually understand mappings, mixins, and the eight different "Minecraft" SDKs.
- Tell the difference between a mod, a plugin, a datapack, and a resource pack — and pick the right one.
